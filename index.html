FAQ

  • Is the game free to play?

    Yes! Honey Bee Park is completely free to download and play. In-app purchases are available for optional cosmetics or time-saving items, but never required

  • Can I play offline?

    Yes. You can fully enjoy the game offline. Progress saves locally and syncs when you’re back online

  • Is there a timer or energy system?

    No timers, no energy. You play at your own pace — it’s part of the cozy game design

  • How many bees can I have?

    You can unlock over 30 bee types, and they each have their own charm and style

  • Are the costumes just for looks?

    Some are purely aesthetic, while others give small passive boosts like faster collection or auto-crafting

  • Can kids play this game?

    Yes, it’s family-friendly, safe, and has no external links or ads. Ideal for players ages 6 and up

  • What’s the goal of the game?

    Build your garden, unlock more bees and facilities, and decorate your paradise! There’s no ‘end,’ just continual growth

  • Is there a competitive mode?

    No, the game is designed for solo relaxation. No rankings, no PvP, just calm

  • Does the game have events?

    Yes! Seasonal gardens and costume drops come throughout the year

  • Can I decorate my garden freely?

    Absolutely! You can place, rotate, and theme your park however you like
